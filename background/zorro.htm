Zorro Bus

The Zorro bus is the standard for expansion cards on the bigger Amiga models since the the Amiga 1000. The 16 bit wide Zorro-II was introduced with the A2000, and Zorro-III had its premiere with the A3000. Zorro-III is 32 bits wide and was one of the fastest bus systems at the time it was released. It's also downwards compatible with the Zorro-II bus. One of the main features of the Zorro bus is Autoconfig.
